I'm currently a Master student at TU Darmstadt. I've held numerous HiWi (TA/RA for Americans) positions, and I'm currently hold a HiWi position at the Bioinspired Communication Systems group. In the past, I've worked at LMKR as a research engineer, as a major part of the development and research for GVERSE Attributes, an application that makes use of Nvidia CUDA to compute and render seismic attributes in real time. In my free time, I work on sparse, a Python library providing multidimensional sparse arrays compatible with Numpy.
